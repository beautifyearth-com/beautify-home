Street Art Scooop #19

Your weekly scooop of street art, opps, events, and media curated by the Beautify Earth team. Since 2012, Beautify Earth has produced, promote, and advocated for art on the streets through local art programs, digital products, and educational resources. Header art by Patrick Maxcy.

    Check out this artist

    Patrick Maxcy
    Denver, CO, US

    Patrick was born and raised in Florida, where the ocean became a source of inspiration for his artwork. Drawing from his personal experiences and the places he has traveled to, Patrick highlights nature and animals as a prominent force in the stories he tells in his paintings, drawings and murals. Through his work, he hopes to spark conversation on disappearing nature and the encroachment of the modern world with subjects and their surroundings depicted in a harmony and balance that they wouldn't normally interact with.

    Support local art programs

    Beautify Earth was built by and for our community with a shared vision of supporting local artists and art initiatives. Our team is committed to not only creating more art in communties but advancing practices of how and why we produce art in public space. Every donation makes a difference and allows us to support more artists and engage more communities. To discuss supporting at a larger scale or partnerships please email hello@beautifyearth.com.
