NEW OPP -- Adobe Max

(this is under a NDA so hush hush please) Moral had an initial meeting with a creative agency last week that is working with Adobe on their annual conference Adobe Max. This event is set to take place in LA in October and they are interested in commissioning three murals in response to the LA fires. We are exploring the possibility of tying this into our current projects in Pasadena, Santa Monica, and Glendale.

blue earth street art

Blue Earth Street Art is an program between BE and Blue Robotics to connect people with underwater ecosystems through local mural & street art initatives

Ocean Business Conference in Southampton, UK

The Blue Robotics team is headed to Edinburgh, UK April 5-7th for some BE/BR bonding with Moral before we all head down to Southampton for the Ocean Business conference where we will be painting three murals. We're working with local artists Pogo and Sian with Moral faciliating the community wall.

Earth Day mural in San Diego

BE and Blue Robotics are partnering with PADI Aware and Neurogum to create a 3000 sqft ocean mural for Earth Day in San Diego. We will be hosting a community day on April 19th that will include a dive at La Jolla cove and community painting opportunities.

CA Seaweed Festival (CSF) in Long Beach

Last years collaboration with CSF was awesome and we're excited to do it again this year as they head to Altasea at the port of Los Angeles in October 10-11th. More details here. We'll soon be looking for walls, local marine orgs, and sponsors in Long Beach so keep your eyes and ears open.

community art

Community walls

community walls are a new initiative to provide open spaces for artists to create original works. This space is great for artists looking to experiment with new ideas, community projects, space to practice, or visiting artists who want to do a quick piece.

  • Venice Blvd -- our first community wall is up and running! Nicole Sherman, Lucretia Torva, and our own Evan Meyer were the first round of artists to use the space and got together on March 16 for a paint day. Big thanks to Jules for overseeing the paint day! This is a quarterly rotation so the next round of artists will be around June 22nd.  
  • next steps -- create landing page, find more walls, spread the word

LA fire murals

  • Daniela is spearheading a new initiative with support from Karin and Sergio to create murals on Montana Ave in Santa Monica and at a school in Pasadena. The initiative was inspired by a mural that withstood the fires at a school in the Palisades. From Daniela "the whole school burned down except for the mural. With the words “you’re stronger than you think” “Be brave” “Find the light”. I want to create and honor this story by painting a similar mural as a symbol of support for our neighbors"
  • upcoming -- create landing page
