Street Art Scooop #20

Your weekly scooop of street art, opps, events, and media curated by the Beautify Earth team. Since 2012, Beautify Earth has produced, promoted, and advocated for art on the streets through local art programs, digital products, and educational resources. Header art by Clyde.

Check out this artist

Clyde
Phoenix, AZ, US

Graffiti-artist Clyde began his journey into the arts in late 2012. His motives primarily influenced from his passion for architecture pushed him to create large works in the public eye. Clyde predominantly creates his works in his hometown Tempe, Arizona where he also graduated from Arizona State University with a bachelor in the arts for design studies. His work is characterized by a combination of realism and skewed patterns creating perplexity in the image and wonder in the tactics.

Support local art programs

Beautify Earth was built by and for our community with a shared vision of supporting local artists and art initiatives. Our team is committed to not only creating more art in communties but advancing practices of how and why we produce art in public space. Every donation makes a difference and allows us to support more artists and engage more communities. To discuss supporting at a larger scale or partnerships please email hello@beautifyearth.com.

  • Santa Monica Street Art: promoting opportunities for artists and the community to engage with and develop street art that reflects the history, spirit, and people of Santa Monica
  • Blue Earth Street Art: connecting communities to marine ecosystems through local mural and street art initiatives around the world
  • Beautify schools: creating murals in schools with local artists to promote arts education
  • BEverse: a digital hub facilitating the creation and exploration of local art, artists, walls, and resources
