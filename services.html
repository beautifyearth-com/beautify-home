Estrella Jalisco and Beautify launched campaign to honor local stars nominated on Twitter by their own neighbors. Children's visions for a better future was the heart of this campaign led by No Kid Hungry.  Working with five local artists across the US, we created murals inspired by the perspective of local children on how they were affected by the pandemic and their vision for a better future. Frequently
Asked
Questions

How do I talk to someone to learn more?

Email us at hello@beautifyearth.com or schedule a call here

How much does a mural cost?

Lots of factors go into the cost of mural project but when it comes to paying artist we recommend a minimum of $35/sqft. Other common costs to keep in mind are management costs, wall costs, and photography/videography. If you have a project in mind, contact us and we’d be happy to put a proposal together for you.

How do we create the mural design?

We work with our clients to develop a creative concept that capture your message or theme. Once a concept is developed we recommend letting the artist take it from there and use their knowledge of art and design to create the original artwork. We facilitate communication between all parties throughout the whole process.

How do we find a wall for our project?

We have wall partners all over the world to host your mural. If you’re looking for a wall where we don’t yet have a partner we are able to research and contact walls in the area in which you’re interested.

What type of clients do you work with?

We work with anyone who is interested in partnering with artists. Big and small companies, cities, nonprofits, corporate real estate, developers, you name it.

How much does it cost to manage our project on the platform?

We try to make the platform as affordable as possible but maintenance and support require us to apply a 3% transaction fee for payments made to artists. We do not take any fees from artists.
