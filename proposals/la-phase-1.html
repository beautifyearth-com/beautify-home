Phase 1 Scope
Co-branded landing page Co-brand identity Wall owner recruitment campaign City of LA boundary mapping Milestone reporting

Phase 1 builds the LA Wall Registry — the verified database of wall owners within City limits, and the backbone of the public-facing LA Mural Grid. Beautify Earth serves as administrative concierge through the ordinance process, driving registrations via BIDs, Chambers, neighborhood associations, community partners, and other stakeholders under the City of LA co-brand.
