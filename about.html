OUR STORY

Since 2012, Beautify Earth has been at the forefront of the street art movement, producing, promoting, and advocating for art on the streets through local mural productions, media, and networks.

What started as a passion project has grown into a global community of artists, organizations, and art lovers working together to make the world more beautiful, one wall at a time.

Today, we operate as both a social enterprise and 501c3 nonprofit, dedicating our resources to creating meaningful art experiences that connect people to their environment and each other.
