MORE THAN MURALS

Beautify Earth is a movement to support independent art and artists

In a world of digital noise, murals provide meaningful experiences that connect people to their environment and each other.

Since 2012, Beautify Earth has supported art on the streets through local mural productions, media, and networks.

We go beyond the paint to create opportunities, processes, and spaces that promote artistic freedom and local cultures.

Interested in supporting our work or partnering to create more independent art? Let's talk.

WHO WE ARE

We're on a mission to connect the world with street art by advocating for art, artist, and vibrant communities.

The Beautify Earth community is driven by our shared vision of creating a more civic, social, and creative society.

Humanity, creativity, and community guide every aspect of our work - centering people and purpose above all else.

YOUR IMPACT BEYOND THE WALL

We are dedicated to re-investing in communities

Beautify Earth operates as a social enterprise and 501c3 nonprofit, dedicating our time, energy, and funds to community art experiences.

We are based in Los Angeles, CA and Edinburgh, UK but work with communities around the world.
